BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value derived from a person's weight and height.
It is used as a simple and quick screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight categories that may indicate potential health risks.

BMI Categories

Underweight: BMI < 18.5

A BMI under 18.5 means you're underweight. This could be due to malnutrition or health issues and may require medical attention.

Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered normal, indicating a healthy balance between your height and weight.

Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9

A BMI from 25 to 29.9 is considered overweight. It suggests that you may be at a higher risk for health problems like heart disease or diabetes.

Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

A BMI of 30 or higher is classified as obesity. It is associated with a significantly increased risk of various health issues, including heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers.
